ABOUT US

Code Vimarsh is a student-driven technical community focused on building strong problem-solving skills and practical development knowledge. The club encourages learning through hands-on practice, peer discussions, and mentorship from seniors and alumni.

Our primary focus areas include Data Structures & Algorithms and collaborative projects that help students grow both technically and professionally.
